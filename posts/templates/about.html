About BH Sticky Notes

Welcome to BH Sticky Notes, your go-to app for managing your notes effortlessly.

BH Sticky Notes is a project created as part of the HyperionDev program. This application allows users to:

  • Create accounts to manage their notes.
  • Write and save sticky notes for various purposes.
  • Edit and update existing notes to keep information current.
  • Delete notes that are no longer needed.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and beautifully styled note-taking experience. We leverage the power of Bootstrap for a modern and responsive design, and Django for a robust and scalable backend.

Thank you for choosing BH Sticky Notes. We hope it helps you stay organised and efficient!
